Adopt Process

    Adopt by email or phone:
        Contact us and our staffs will tell you what to do next.
        Email - lly_adoption@gmail.com
        Phone - 123-456-7890

    Adopt online:
        1. Create an account of our web and log into our web.
        2. Browse pets on our web and find available pets you are interested to adopt.
        3. Click the "Adopt" button on your interested pets' page.
        4. Fill out the application form and come to our shelter at the appointed time.
        NOTE: If you are interested in more than one pet, you must fill out an application form for every pet you are interested in.
